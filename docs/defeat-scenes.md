# Inevitable Eternity — Chapter Defeat Scenes

> Plays when all player units are petrified or captured.
>
> All characters in this document are female — the Queen, her officers, every named and unnamed
> figure on both sides. No exceptions.
>
> **The Queen's Designation**: She sees the object before the stone sets. Her designation speech
> names what the character already IS — not what they will be assigned. The stone finalizes on
> her word; armor and equipment crumble in the same instant. Every named character has a last
> line before the stone completes. The last line is always too short.
>
> The Queen is the primary inspector in all chapters.
>
> **Scene structure**: Queen encounters character → observes briefly, already seeing the object
> → names the designation → stone sets (armor crumbles simultaneously) → character's last words
> → Queen moves on.
>
> **NPC treatment**: every chapter has NPC petrifications. Noted in setting blocks, assigned in
> bulk. They are already architecture before the Queen arrives.
>
> Action beats in italics. Designation speech in quotes. Last words in bold.
